## Solutions

2DGame engines in Lua language could be named just a few:

- [LÖVE](https://love2d.org/), the most popular 2D game engine in Lua.
- [Solar2D](https://solar2d.com/), focus on ease of iterations and usage.
- [Gideros](http://giderosmobile.com/), an excellent toolset building cross-platform games.

## Why choose LÖVE

- Light weight
- Easy to get started
- Beginner Friendly [API](http://love2d-community.github.io/love-api/)
- Choosen by [HarvardX](https://www.edx.org/professional-certificate/harvardx-computer-science-for-game-development)
